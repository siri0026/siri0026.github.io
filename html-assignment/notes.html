HTML Tags

<p>
Defines a paragraph of text.
<h1> - <h6>
Represents importance of headings, from <h1> (most important and largest heading) to <h6> (least important and smallest heading).
<sub>
Used to display text as subscript.
<ul> and <ol>
Used to create unordered (bulleted) and ordered (numbered) lists.
<a>
An anchor tag used to link to websites, pages, images, documents or any other resource on the web or our server.
